Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process my order after submission?

We typically process orders within 24 hours of submission. During peak seasons, it may take up to 48 hours. You'll receive a confirmation email once your order has been processed.

What information do I need to provide in the order form?

You'll need to provide: your full name, shipping address, contact information, product details (links, names, sizes, colors, quantities), and any special instructions. The more detailed your information, the smoother the ordering process.

Can I modify my order after submission?

You can modify your order within 2 hours of submission by contacting our support team. After that, changes may not be possible as we begin processing orders quickly to ensure timely delivery.

What if an item I want is out of stock?

If an item becomes unavailable after you submit your order, we'll contact you with alternative options. You can choose a different item, size, or color, or receive a refund for that particular product.

How will I know the status of my order?

You'll receive email updates at each stage of the process. Once your items ship, you'll get tracking information. You can also check your order status by logging into your DivaCart account.
